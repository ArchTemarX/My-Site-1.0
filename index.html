Who Am I?

I’m a frontend enthusiast with a background in economics and logistics.
After completing my Master’s in Finance in Ukraine, I spent several years working in banks before moving to Poland, where I’ve been working in logistics for the past 6 years.
Over time, I discovered a strong passion for web development. I’ve been learning frontend technologies on my own and now I’m focused on turning this hobby into a full-time career.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me at the gym, gaming, or rollerblading around the city.

What I Do

  • Crafting Unique Websites

    I enjoy the process of creating websites. Each new project is an opportunity for me to learn something new, whether it’s about design, functionality, or coding. Watching a website come to life and seeing how it works for users is really satisfying. I'm always excited to continue growing and exploring new ways to improve.

  • Creating Intuitive Experiences

    I’m also passionate about building adaptive websites that work seamlessly across devices. Ensuring that users have a consistent and smooth experience, whether on mobile or desktop, is something I prioritize in every project. As I continue to expand my knowledge in web design and user experience, I’m committed to making the websites I build even more user-friendly, functional, and accessible to everyone.

  • Constantly Evolving

    Web development is always changing, and I’m excited to be a part of that. I enjoy learning about new technologies and how they can help improve the way I build websites. Every time I learn something new, it makes me want to push myself further and apply what I’ve learned to my projects. Coding has become a passion, and I’m committed to learning and adapting every day.
